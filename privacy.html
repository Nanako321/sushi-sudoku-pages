Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2026

Overview

Sushi Sudoku ("the App") is a casual puzzle game that combines Sudoku gameplay with a sushi collection experience.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

Information We Collect

Sushi Sudoku does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The App does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, location information, contacts, or other personal data.

Game Data Storage

Game progress, completed levels, unlocked sushi collections, and game settings are stored locally on your device.

This information remains on your device and is not uploaded to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Sushi Sudoku does not use third-party advertising services, analytics services, tracking technologies, or data collection tools.

Children's Privacy

Sushi Sudoku does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App can be used without providing any personal information.

Data Security

Because Sushi Sudoku does not collect personal information, we do not store or process personal data on our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the App's features or privacy practices change.

Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
