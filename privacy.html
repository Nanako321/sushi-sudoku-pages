Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2026

Overview

Sushi Sudoku ("the App") is a puzzle game that combines Sudoku gameplay with a sushi collection experience.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information.

Information We Collect

Sushi Sudoku does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The App does not require account registration, and we do not collect your name, email address, location information, contacts, or other personal data.

Game Data and Local Storage

Game progress, unlocked sushi collections, completed levels, and settings are stored locally on your device.

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Sushi Sudoku currently does not use third-party advertising services, analytics services, tracking technologies, or data collection tools.

If third-party services are added in future updates, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Purchases

If you purchase additional content or unlock the full game, payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store.

We do not collect, access, or store your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Sushi Sudoku does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App can be enjoyed without providing personal information.

Data Security

Because Sushi Sudoku does not collect personal information, we do not store personal data on external servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable requirements.

Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
