Thank you for playing Sushi Sudoku. Please review the frequently asked questions below or contact us if you need assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Sushi Sudoku include in-app purchases or subscriptions?

No. Sushi Sudoku is a paid app and does not include in-app purchases, subscriptions, or advertisements.

Where is my game progress stored?

Game progress, completed levels, unlocked sushi collections, certifications, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Will deleting the App remove my progress?

Deleting the App may also remove game data stored locally on your device. Please consider this before uninstalling the App.

I found a bug or encountered a problem.

Please email us and include your device model, iOS version, App version, and a description of the issue. Screenshots are also helpful.
