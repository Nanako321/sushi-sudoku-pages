Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Sushi Sudoku, go to the purchase screen, and tap “Restore Purchase.” Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple Account used for the original purchase.

Where is my game progress stored?

Game progress and unlocked sushi collections are stored locally on your device.

I found a bug or encountered a problem.

Please email us and include your device model, iOS version, and a description of the issue. Screenshots are also helpful.
